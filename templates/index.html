About

Built for clarity under pressure

PacketVision is a lightweight network sniffer and analysis dashboard designed to make raw traffic understandable. It pairs a Python & Flask backend with Scapy-powered capture and a modern, responsive interface — turning a firehose of packets into clear, actionable insight.

Whether you are learning how networks behave, debugging a stubborn connection, or watching for suspicious activity, PacketVision keeps the signal front and centre.
